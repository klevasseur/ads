Item 5.1.4.7.b.
Express the following systems of equations using matrices:
  1. \(\displaystyle \begin{array}{c} 2 x_1- x_2= 4\\ x_1+ x_2= 0\\ \end{array}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \begin{array}{c} x_1+ x_2+ 2 x_3= 1\\ x_1+ 2 x_2-x_3= -1\\ x_1+ 3 x_2+x_3= 5\\ \end{array}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \begin{array}{c} x_1+x_2\quad\quad= 3\\ \quad \quad x_2\quad\quad= 5\\ x_1 \quad \quad+ 3x_3= 6\\ \end{array} \)
