Note that if \(j\) is down the one-way street from \(i\text{,}\) then \(w(i, j) < w(j, i)\text{.}\) The values of \(C_{opt}\text{,}\) and \(C_{cn}\) are 20 and 32, respectively. Verify that \(C_{cn}\) is 32 by using the closest neighbor algorithm. The value of \(\frac{C_{cn}}{C_{opt}} = 1.6\) is significant in this case since our salesman would spend 60 percent more time on the road if he used the closest neighbor algorithm.
