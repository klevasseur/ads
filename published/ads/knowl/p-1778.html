The first two operations we introduce are very natural and are not likely cause much confusion. The first is matrix addition. It seems natural that if \(A =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 2 & -1 \\ \end{array} \right)\) and \(B =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ -5 & 2 \\ \end{array} \right)\) , then
\begin{equation*} A + B =\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1+3 & 0+4 \\ 2-5 & -1+2 \\ \end{array} \right)=\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 4 \\ -3 & 1 \\ \end{array} \right). \end{equation*}
