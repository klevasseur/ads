They are all lined up! Now look at \(p(1)\text{:}\) The sum of the positive integers from 1 to 1 is \(\frac{1\cdot(1+1)}{2}\text{.}\) Clearly, \(p(1)\) is true. This sets off a chain reaction. Since \(p(1) \Rightarrow p(2)\text{,}\) \(p(2)\) is true. Since \(p(2) \Rightarrow p(3)\text{,}\) \(p(3)\) is true; and so on. \(\blacksquare\)
