• \(\displaystyle \mathcal{P}(\emptyset )=\{\emptyset \}\)
  • \(\displaystyle \mathcal{P}(\{1\}) = \{\emptyset , \{1\}\}\)
  • \(\mathcal{P}(\{1,2\}) = \{\emptyset , \{1\}, \{2\}, \{1, 2\}\}\text{.}\)
