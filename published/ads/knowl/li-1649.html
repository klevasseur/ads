Item 3.
Repeat:
  1. If possible, find a flow augmenting path with respect to \(f_i\text{.}\)
  2. If a flow augmenting path exists, then:
    1. Determine
      \begin{equation*} \begin{split} d & = \min \{\{w(e) - f_i(e) \mid e \text{ is used in the forward direction}\},\\ & \quad \quad \quad \{f_i(e) \mid e \text{ is used in the reverse direction}\}\}\\ \\ \end{split} \end{equation*}
    2. Define \(f_{i+1}\) by
      \begin{equation*} \begin{array}{cc} f_{i+1}(e) = f_i(e) & \text{if } e \text{ is not part of the flow augmenting path} \\ f_{i+1}(e)=f_i(e)+d & \text{if } e \text{ is used in the forward direction} \\ f_{i+1}(e)=f_i(e)-d & \text{if } e \text{ is used in the reverse direction} \\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
    3. \(\displaystyle i = i + 1\)
until no flow augmenting path exists.
